Fake News Detection

I worked in a team to explore multiple AI techniques to identify
unreliable news using Python.

We attempted to utilize AI techniques to identify news into 4 categories: “Satire”, “Hoax”, “Propaganda” and “Reliable News” to help combat unreliable news. Basic techniques used for fake news detection such as Naive Bayes and Logistic Regression only yield a F1 Score of 0.6826

I used Python to run the AI models for testing, mainly the transformers library. I mainly focused on XLNet, which aims to improve on the BERT model by enabling bidirectional context. After applying pre-processing and feature engineering, the F1 Score of XLNet was 0.71. I also worked on ensembling with different models, which produced a final score of .77.
